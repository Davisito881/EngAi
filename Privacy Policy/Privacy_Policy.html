Privacy Policy & Data Protection Agreement

This document constitutes a legally binding agreement between you and EngAI regarding data sovereignty, processing protocols, and privacy standards.

Effective Date: February 23, 2026 Jurisdiction: International / EU Compliant

1. Preamble and Scope of Applicability

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") governs the collection, processing, usage, and storage of data by the EngAI Application (hereinafter "The Platform" or "Service"). By accessing, installing, or utilizing the neural processing capabilities of The Platform, you (the "User") acknowledge that you have read, understood, and consented to the data practices described herein.

This Policy is drafted in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and global standards for data integrity in engineering software.

2. Definitions and Interpretation

  • "Personal Data" shall mean any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • "Structural Metadata" refers to the non-personal technical parameters, load cases, and geometry data input into the AI engine.
  • "Neural Compute" refers to the processing of data through our proprietary Transformer-based models housed on secure cloud instances.
  • "Data Controller" refers to the entity (EngAI) determining the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.

3. Categories of Information Collected

To facilitate high-fidelity engineering simulations, The Platform collects specific categories of data through automated protocols:

3.1. Technical Telemetry & Device Fingerprinting

We automatically capture device-specific information, including but not limited to: Internet Protocol (IP) address, operating system version, CPU architecture (for optimization of local vs. cloud inference), and unique device identifiers (UUID).

3.2. Voluntarily Provided Information

Upon registration, the User provides identity verification data such as: professional credentials, institutional affiliation (e.g., UPB, Romaero), and billing instruments processed via Stripe's encrypted API.

4. Artificial Intelligence & Neural Processing

Transparency Note: EngAI utilizes advanced Machine Learning algorithms. While your structural inputs are processed to generate results, your proprietary blueprints are isolated in encrypted containers.

The Platform employs AI components to optimize structural calculations. Data submitted for simulation is processed ephemerally. We utilize aggregated, anonymized vector embeddings to refine the accuracy of our global model, strictly ensuring that no specific project intellectual property is reconstructible from the training data.

5. Geolocation Protocols

The Platform may access coarse location data (non-GPS precise) derived from network signals to:

  • Comply with regional taxation laws (VAT/Sales Tax determination via Stripe).
  • Route your connection to the nearest low-latency computation node (Edge Computing).
  • Ensure compliance with international export control regulations for engineering software.

6. Third-Party Data Processors

We do not sell your data. We share data strictly with infrastructure partners required to operate the service. These partners are vetted for SOC2 and ISO 27001 compliance:

Stripe Inc. Payment Processing & Fraud Detection
Google Firebase Authentication & Real-time Database
AWS / Google Cloud Neural Computation Infrastructure

7. Security Infrastructure

EngAI employs defense-in-depth security strategies including:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data is transmitted via TLS 1.3 tunnels with 256-bit AES encryption.
  • Encryption at Rest: Project archives are encrypted on disk using user-specific keys.
  • Access Control: Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures only authorized daemons can access simulation queues.

8. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under applicable international law, you possess the following rights regarding your data sovereignty:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all raw data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccuracies in your profile.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Mandate the permanent destruction of your account and associated project archives.
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/XML).

9. Data Retention Policy

We retain User data only as long as necessary to fulfill the operational purposes. Upon subscription termination, simulation logs are deleted after 30 days. Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years solely for tax audit compliance obligations.
